Audio, Recording & Streaming

You've mastered the visuals, now let's talk about sound and sharing your creation. This section covers the Audio Mixer and the Output controls. Click each topic to expand and learn more.

Sound is half the battle! The Audio Mixer gives you control over every audio source.

  • Volume Faders: Drag up/down to control loudness.
  • Mute Button (Speaker Icon): Instantly silence an input.
  • Solo Button (Headphone Icon): Listen to a single source in your headphones to check it.
  • Audio Meters: Visual bars that show volume. Aim for green, touch yellow, avoid red (distortion!).
  • Golden Rule: Always monitor your audio with headphones!

Transitions are how you switch between shots professionally.

  • Cut: An instant, sharp switch. Good for fast-paced content.
  • Fade: A smooth, gentle dissolve. Your go-to for most situations.
  • Merge: A slightly more dynamic, elegant dissolve.
  • Duration: Controls how fast or slow the transition happens.

Once your show is ready, here's how you get it out to the world. Always check settings with the gear icon (⚙️) first!

  • Record: Saves a video file of your live show to your computer.
  • Stream: Sends your show to online platforms like YouTube, Facebook, or Twitch.
  • External: Sends your video feed to another monitor or projector.
  • Full Screen: Displays your program output on a dedicated monitor.
